Types of Combinator Selector
  1. Decendent: Target child as well as nested child, separated by space
  2. Child: Target only child, represented by ">".
  3. General Sibling:Target all siblings after closing tag, represented by (~)
  4. Adjacent Sibling: Target Sibling just after the closing tag. represented by (+)
